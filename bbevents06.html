Sept. 7-11 - Dubuque, Iowa

Several members of the Breast Buddies team went to the Dubuque International Dragon Boat Festival
held on September 9 and 10. We were invited to paddle with the International Breast of Friends
team of Dubuque. We one of two survivor teams, the other being the Fighting Angels Abreast.
Our first two races were against mixed teams but on Sunday the two survivor teams raced against
each other. It was a very close race but the Breast of Friends team won the gold!!! Our host team
showed us great hospitality and took us on some very enjoyable sightseeing trips. We all had a great time!!!
